## 3. Feature Catalog

**Video Agent:** Prompt-based video generation. Type an idea, get a share-ready video.

**AI Avatars:** Photo Avatar (single image → talking video), Public Avatar (stock library), Digital Twin (your likeness cloned).

**Avatar V:** Latest avatar engine — most lifelike yet. Motion/gesture control, generate looks, look packs, custom voice emotion.

**AI Studio:** Text-based video editor. Control tone, delivery, gestures, emotion.

**Video Translation:** Translate videos into 175+ languages with voice cloning and lip-sync. Preserves voice, tone, pacing.

**Product Placement:** Upload product image + script → AI places product in avatar's hand with music, captions, voiceovers.

**UGC Ads / Reaction Videos:** Create social content without a camera. Product reviews, news reactions, story-style videos.

**Third-party AI Models:** Access Sora, Veo, Kling for B-roll; Flux for images; ElevenLabs for speech.

**Interactive Video:** Quizzes, links, branching, decisioning (Business+).

**SCORM Export:** For LMS integration (Business+).

**API:** Pay-as-you-go for video generation, translation, text-to-speech.
